# Def: Condition Number

Let $A \in \mathbb{K}^{n\times n}$ be regular (or invertible), i.e., $\det A \ne 0$. Let $\lVert\cdot\rVert$ be a norm on $\mathbb{K}^{n}$.

Then,

$$
\operatorname{cond}_{\lVert\cdot\rVert}(A) := \lVert A\rVert\,\lVert A^{-1}\rVert
$$

is called **condition number** (of $A$ with respect to $\lVert\cdot\rVert$).
