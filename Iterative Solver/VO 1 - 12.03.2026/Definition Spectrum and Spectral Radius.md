# Def: Spectrum and Spectral Radius

For a matrix $A \in \mathbb{K}^{n\times n}$, let

$$
\sigma(A) := \{d_1,\dots,d_n\}
$$

be the set of eigenvalues, i.e., the so-called **spectrum** of $A$.

Moreover,

$$
\rho(A) := \max_{j=1,\dots,n} |d_j|
$$

denotes the so-called **spectral radius** of $A$.
