Sei $N=1$. Betrachte das *Dirichlet-Integral* $\mathcal{F}(u):=\frac{1}{2}\int_{\Omega}|\nabla u|^2 dx$, also $F(x,z,p)=\frac{1}{2}|p|^2 \to \frac{\partial F}{\partial z}=0, \frac{\partial F}{\partial p_{j}}=p_{j}$.
Dann ist $\delta \mathcal{F}(u, \xi)= \int_{\Omega}\sum_{j=1}^n \frac{\partial u}{\partial x_{j}} \frac{\partial \xi}{\partial x_{j}}dx=\int_{\Omega}\nabla u \cdot \nabla \xi dx$.
