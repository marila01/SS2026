Sei $N=1$. Oberfläche $O(u)=\int_{\Omega}\sqrt{ 1+|\nabla u|^2 }dx$, also $F(x,z,p)=\sqrt{ 1+ |p|^2 }$.
Dann gilt
$$
\frac{\partial F}{\partial z}=0, \quad \frac{\partial F}{\partial p_{j}}=\frac{p_{j}}{\sqrt{ 1+|p|^2 }}  
$$
$$
\implies \delta O(u)=\int_{\Omega} \frac{\nabla u \cdot \nabla \xi}{\sqrt{ 1+|\nabla u|^2 }}dx
$$
